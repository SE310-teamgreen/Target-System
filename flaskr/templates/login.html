Welcome

Welcome to our reengineering of the SWENET and EdRepo systems prototype!

Target System is a reengineering effort of the SWENET and EdRepo applications.


The purpose of Target System is to make it easier for people to store, find, and use educational information related to a particular field of study. Target System aims to do this by providing a centralized place to store this information so that the authors and the users of the educational content only need to visit one place to find it all. By providing a platform for storing educational materials and information, the application thus also helps people to share knowledge in an accessible and easy-to-find way. To improve ease of access, the target system also aims to standardize the format of the educational information presented on the application by providing a way of discretizing it and relating it to a certain topic. This is done by storing all educational information on the application in the form of “modules”. A particular subset of information within a field of study, like for example information related to the Software Engineering topic of design patterns, is stored and presented as a module. This module contains an aggregation of related educational information and activities, such as exercises or readings. For example, such information could consist of a set of lecture slides on the Connector pattern in this scenario. By aggregating educational content and relating them to particular topics, the application allows its users to search through educational information more effectively. Lastly, the target system also is a platform for improving the quality of educational information available and encouraging discussion. The application facilitates moderation of content added to the application’s repository by providing a process for reviewing and editing the content added by the application’s administrative personnel. Additionally, the application allows for the administrative personnel to control the group of users who can submit content to the application and who is responsible for reviewing the submitted content. The application also allows for users of the application to provide ratings to materials so that they may provide feedback to the authors and other users of the quality of the educational content.
